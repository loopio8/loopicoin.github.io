Loopi Coin Tokenomics Explained

Here’s how we’re structuring Loopi Coin’s tokenomics to ensure sustainability and fairness for our community:

1️⃣

Total Supply

The total supply of Loopi Coin is 688,866,674,258 tokens, ensuring ample availability while maintaining scarcity to encourage long-term value growth.

2️⃣

Distribution Plan

  • 80% for Sacrificers (551,093,339,406 tokens): Allocated to those who join the Loopi Sacrifice, rewarding early supporters of our freedom of speech movement.
  • 10% for Liquidity Pool (68,886,667,426 tokens): Reserved to establish a strong liquidity pool on exchanges, enabling seamless trading from day one.
  • 5% for Development and Ecosystem Growth (34,443,333,713 tokens): Used to enhance the Loopi ecosystem through technical advancements and product development.
  • 5% for Marketing and Partnerships (34,443,333,713 tokens): Dedicated to global marketing campaigns, partnerships, and community-building efforts to expand Loopi’s reach.
3️⃣

Token Utility

  • Decentralized Transactions: Fast and secure payments.
  • Governance: Token holders will have voting rights to shape Loopi’s future.
  • Rewards: Incentives for community participation and contributions.
4️⃣

Anti-Whale and Fairness Mechanisms

  • Transaction Limits: Caps on maximum tokens traded per transaction to prevent manipulation.
  • Gradual Unlocking: Team and development funds unlock over time to ensure long-term commitment.
5️⃣

Commitment to Reinvestment

On launch day, 80% of sacrifice funds will be reinvested to maximize benefits for our community, focusing on liquidity, marketing, and growth initiatives.
